Frequently Asked Questions

What is Book Tracker?

Book Tracker is a platform designed to help you manage and track your reading habits, organize your book collection, and discover new books.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling out the registration form with your details.

Is Book Tracker free to use?

Yes, Book Tracker offers a free plan with essential features. Additional premium features may be available through a subscription.

How can I reset my password?

If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

Can I share my book collection with others?

Yes, you can share your book collection with friends by enabling the sharing option in your account settings.

How do I contact support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact us at info@booktracker.com.
