Frequently Asked Questions

"Here are the answers to some of the most common questions about BookTracker."

What is BookTracker?

BookTracker is a platform designed to help you manage and track your reading habits, organize your book collection, and discover new books.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling out the registration form with your details.

Is BookTracker free to use?

Yes, BookTracker offers a free plan with essential features. Additional premium features may be available through a subscription.

How can I reset my password?

If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

Can I share my book collection with others?

Yes, you can share books that you have read or plan on reading with your friends in our community page.

How do I contact support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact us at info@booktracker.com or reach us here
